Conference Themes

The conference will be focused on the various direction of development in green technology revolution. Topics of interest include but not limited to the following subjects.


1. Renewable & green energy sources
2. Energy Generation & Storage Technologies
3. Energy uses reduction and conservation
4. Smart Systems and Smart Infrastructures
5. Optimization techniques for energy applications
6. Forecasting techniques and predictions
7. Environmental, legal, social, economic, and political Impacts
